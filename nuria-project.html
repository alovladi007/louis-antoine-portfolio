Nuria AI

Reasoning AI for OBGYN and Natal Care Support

Project Overview

Nuria AI is a specialized reasoning-focused Large Language Model designed to support obstetrics, gynecology, and natal care professionals. This AI assistant provides evidence-based insights for maternal health, prenatal care decisions, and women's reproductive health, helping healthcare providers deliver personalized and informed care throughout pregnancy and beyond.

Project Under Development

This maternal health AI system is currently in the planning and research phase. Updates coming soon!

Core Capabilities

Prenatal Care Planning

Personalized care plans based on patient history and risk factors

Risk Assessment

Early identification of pregnancy complications and high-risk conditions

Growth Monitoring

Track fetal development and maternal health indicators

Medication Safety

Evidence-based guidance on medication use during pregnancy and lactation

Care Timeline

Automated scheduling and reminders for prenatal appointments and tests

Patient Education

Clear, accessible information for expectant mothers and families

Safety & Ethical Considerations

Nuria AI is designed with the highest standards of medical ethics and patient safety. The system emphasizes:

  • Evidence-based recommendations from peer-reviewed research
  • Cultural sensitivity and personalized care approaches
  • Clear communication of uncertainty and limitations
  • Strict patient privacy and HIPAA compliance
  • Emphasis on supporting, not replacing, clinical judgment
