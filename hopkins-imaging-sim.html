Hopkins Method Overview

The Hopkins method decomposes partially coherent imaging into a sum of coherent systems using Transmission Cross Coefficients (TCCs). This enables fast and accurate aerial image calculation.

I(x,y) = ∫∫ TCC(f₁,f₂) · M(f₁) · M*(f₂) · exp[2πi(f₁-f₂)·(x,y)] df₁df₂
