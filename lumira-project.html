LUMIRA

Large Language Model for Brain Behavior and Neurological Diseases

Project Overview

LUMIRA is a specialized Large Language Model designed to understand and analyze brain behavior patterns and neurological diseases. This advanced AI system aims to support neurologists, neuroscientists, and mental health professionals by providing insights into complex neural patterns, disease progression, and behavioral correlations.

Project Under Development

This neuroscience AI platform is currently in the conceptual and research phase. More details coming soon!

Core Capabilities

EEG/fMRI Analysis

Advanced pattern recognition in neuroimaging data and brain signals

Disease Progression Modeling

Predictive models for neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's

Behavioral Correlation

Link brain activity patterns to behavioral and cognitive outcomes

Treatment Response Prediction

AI-driven insights for personalized treatment planning

Connectome Analysis

Map and analyze brain connectivity networks

Cognitive Assessment

Comprehensive cognitive function evaluation and tracking

Research Focus Areas

  • Neurodegenerative diseases (Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, ALS)
  • Psychiatric disorders (Depression, Schizophrenia, Bipolar)
  • Traumatic brain injury and recovery patterns
  • Developmental disorders (Autism, ADHD)
  • Sleep disorders and circadian rhythms
  • Epilepsy and seizure prediction
