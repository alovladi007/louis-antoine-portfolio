Olmosa Vision

LLM for Medical Imaging Assistant Analysis

Project Overview

Olmosa Vision is an advanced Large Language Model designed specifically for medical imaging analysis. This AI assistant helps radiologists and medical professionals analyze complex medical images, understand findings, and provide evidence-based insights. The system combines state-of-the-art computer vision with natural language processing to bridge the gap between visual data and clinical interpretation.

Project Under Development

This medical AI system is currently in the research and development phase. Check back for updates!

Core Capabilities

Image Analysis Guide

Learn best practices for medical image interpretation with AI-guided tutorials

Pathology Reference

Access comprehensive pathology database with visual examples and explanations

Report Templates

Generate standardized radiology reports with structured findings

Multi-Modal Analysis

Support for CT, MRI, X-ray, ultrasound, and other imaging modalities

Anomaly Detection

AI-powered identification of abnormalities and critical findings

Educational Mode

Interactive learning environment for medical students and residents

Medical AI Safety & Ethics

This system is designed as a clinical decision support tool and will include robust safety measures, explainable AI features, and compliance with medical device regulations. Human oversight remains essential for all diagnostic decisions.
