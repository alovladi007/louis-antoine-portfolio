Academic Social Platform

A verified social platform designed exclusively for the academic and research community. AGORA Hub combines rigorous institutional verification with powerful collaboration tools, AI-powered research assistance, and seamless integration with academic repositories to create a trusted space for scholarly discourse and innovation.

Platform Overview

AGORA Research Hub revolutionizes academic networking by creating a verified, feature-rich environment where researchers, students, and professors can collaborate authentically. Unlike mainstream social networks, AGORA requires institutional verification, supports academic content formats like LaTeX, and provides specialized tools for research collaboration, peer review, and knowledge sharing.
