Metamaterial Fabrication Process Guide

1. E-beam Lithography

Substrate Preparation

• Silicon or quartz substrate cleaning with piranha solution

• Spin-coating of PMMA resist (200-500nm thickness)

• Pre-bake at 180°C for 90 seconds

Pattern Writing

• Beam current: 100-500 pA for sub-100nm features

• Dose: 200-400 μC/cm² depending on resist thickness

• Proximity effect correction for dense patterns

Development

• MIBK:IPA (1:3) developer for 60 seconds

• IPA rinse and N₂ blow dry

• Oxygen plasma descum (optional)

2. Metal Deposition

E-beam Evaporation

• Base pressure: <1×10⁻⁶ Torr

• Deposition rate: 0.5-1 Å/s for gold/silver

• Adhesion layer: 2-5nm Ti or Cr

Lift-off Process

• Acetone soak at 50°C with gentle agitation

• Sonication for stubborn areas (low power)

• Final rinse in IPA and DI water

3. Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Milling

Direct Writing

• Ga⁺ ion beam at 30 kV for milling

• Current: 10-100 pA for precision features

• Real-time SEM imaging for process monitoring

4. Quality Control

Characterization Methods

• SEM imaging for dimensional verification

• AFM for surface roughness measurement

• Optical spectroscopy for resonance verification
