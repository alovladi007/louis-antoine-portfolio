Cloak Design Specifications

Radial Permittivity:
εᵣ(r) = (r - a)/r × (b/(b-a))²

Tangential Permittivity:
εθ(r) = r/(r - a)

Axial Permittivity:
εz(r) = (b/(b-a))² × (r-a)/r
Radial Permeability:
μᵣ(r) = (r - a)/r × (b/(b-a))²

Tangential Permeability:
μθ(r) = r/(r - a)

Axial Permeability:
μz(r) = (b/(b-a))² × (r-a)/r
where: a = inner radius = 1.0λ, b = outer radius = 3.0λ
