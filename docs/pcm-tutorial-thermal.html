Introduction

Thermal management is critical for PCM performance, reliability, and energy efficiency. This advanced tutorial covers thermal modeling, heat dissipation strategies, thermal crosstalk mitigation, and optimization techniques for high-density PCM arrays. You'll learn to balance thermal isolation for programming with heat dissipation for reliability.

Key Concepts

  • Thermal resistance and capacitance modeling
  • Heat dissipation pathways in PCM cells
  • Thermal crosstalk in dense arrays
  • Thermal boundary resistance (TBR)
  • Optimization for low-power operation

Optimization Strategies

Best Practices

  1. Use thermal barriers (SiO₂, Al₂O₃) to reduce crosstalk
  2. Optimize heater geometry for uniform heating
  3. Implement thermal compensation in pulse design
  4. Consider thermoelectric effects in scaled devices
  5. Use thermal simulations to guide layout design
